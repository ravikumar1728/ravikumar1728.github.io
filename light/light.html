I used the Quantum Langevin Equation QLE approach to calculate nonlinear light propagation through one-dimensional (1D) interacting quantum lattice models connected to photon baths at the boundaries. The QLE approach for nonequilibrium transport is an extension of the Heisenberg-Langevin equation approach to nonequilibrium open quantum systems. We write Quantum Langevin Equation corresponding to the system and use these to describe the system properties.
